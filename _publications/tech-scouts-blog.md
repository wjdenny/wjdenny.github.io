In addition to showcases and demonstrations, I also created a tutorial video and blog post on how to integrate Remind with our Desire2Learn LMS (Talon). Remind is a webapp that makes sending announcements to students by SMS easier. I began using this app because our most of our students lacked basic computer skills, were unfamiliar with email, but all had phones that they either knew how to use or had family members that could assist them.

From this little project I learned how to use screen capturing and recording software (in this case, Screencastify on Chromebook) and basic video editing to create these tutorial videos. I also got a little practice writing about design philosophy and technical procedures to a more general audience.
