KCELT is Kirkwood's Center for Excellence in Learning and Teaching, and its mission is to design, develop, and deliver training for all employees. I developed this workshop, completing a program to become a [Duolingo Certified Educator] in the process.

This workshop was the first of several training sessions I organized and facilitated through KCELT. You may view the [slides] from this presentation on this site.

[Duolingo] is a free language learning platform that can support learning outside the classroom. [Duolingo for Schools] provides tools to view student progress and set goals. I had implemented this service in my English Language Acquisition courses and in private tutoring. This workshop provided a broad overview of Duolingo, and Duolingo for Schools, and gave hands-on experience with the service from both student and teacher perspectives. Participants were also be provided with a sample lesson plan and materials. I used a [Doodle] poll to organize attendees and solicit preferences for dates, time, and location of the workshop. The learning objectives of the workshop were:

* Understand the benefits of games in language learning 
* Identify the key features of Duolingo
* Understand how Duolingo (for Schools) works
* Explore ways to use Duolingo in language courses
