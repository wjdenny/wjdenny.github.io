I worked with my colleague, Molly Kelley, to present research on questions related to the trend of plagiarism commited by international students. The research centered on the argument that it is a mistake for Western academics to expect international students to fully understand our standards of academic honesty without explicit training. We outlined a few techniques for developing this awareness and linguistic techniques for avoiding plagiarism in tandem to give students the tools they need to overcome this hurdle. We wrote a [paper](http://midtesol.org/docs/MIDTESOL_Proceedings_2012.pdf#page=88) that was published in the conference proceedings the following year.
