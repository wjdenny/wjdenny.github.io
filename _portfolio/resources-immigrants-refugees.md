In the summer of 2016, I began working on a functional directory of free and low cost resources from non-profits in the area that serve immigrants and refugees. Orginally, it was compiled manually and hosted on [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1EjUjH2K3kxGGxW24Bo98vRCNDxFYLBHk60y_asuiR3I/edit?usp=sharing) and shared with local teachers, social workers, and administrators to help them serve our new residents. Recently, I've made moves to divest myself from Google's infrastructure, and began porting the document to [GitHub](https://github.com/wjdenny/resources-johnson-county) to take advantage of contemporary publishing tools to generate multiple versions of the document while allowing collaborators to edit the underlying information. It is currently a work in progress.
