Zhejiang University's International Campus is a relatively new campus,
following a new model of Sino-foreign cooperative universities where two
high-ranked foreign institutions, the University of Edinburgh and the
University of Illinois-Urbana Champaign, bring their curriclum and expertise to
the Chinese university campus. Courses are taught in English by a majority
international professors.

## Integrated English
I taught several sections of a STEM-focused integrated skills course for
undergraduate students in biomedical sciences and engineering majors. Class
sizes were small, about 12 per section. There were 75 students in total that I
worked with for 16 weeks, on average 10.67 hours per week.

Lessons covered included: pronunciation, intercultural communication
strategies, scientific writing, US/UK culture and current events, and study
skills.

## TOEFL Workshop
In addition to this, I co-developed and taught a TOEFL workshop series. This
helped dual-enrolled seniors meet the TOEFL requirements of their foreign
partner institution. 

## ZIBS iMBA Advanced Graduate English
Lastly, I assisted with a weekend business English program for incoming MBA
students of Zhejiang University's International Master in Business
Administration program. This included twice-monthly one-on-one follow-up
consultations on English acquisition.
